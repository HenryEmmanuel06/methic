Who We Are

Delivering Trusted Power And Energy Solutions For A Sustainable Future

Methic Engineering Limited is a certified engineering service company specializing in Power Support Solutions.
Established in 2017, we deliver design, procurement, supply, installation, and maintenance of renewable energy systems, diesel generators, inverters, HVAC, and safety infrastructure across Nigeria. Our engineers are COREN, NSE, HSE, and PMP certified, bringing technical depth and hands-on experience to every project.
