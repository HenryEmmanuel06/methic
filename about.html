About us

Methic Engineering Limited (MEL) is a registered Nigerian engineering service company, established in February 2017. We specialize in Power Support Solutions, delivering services in design, procurement, supply, installation, and maintenance of renewable energy systems, diesel generators, HVAC systems, fire protection, inverters, and complete power infrastructure. With a strong presence across the South-West, South-East, and South-South regions of Nigeria, we have built a proven track record in power systems, mechanical services, and industrial installations. Our team consists of COREN, NSE, HSE, PMP, and DPR-certified engineers, ensuring every project meets safety, compliance, and performance standards.
